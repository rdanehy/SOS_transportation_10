Our Vision of the Future

Look into our crystal ball

Take a seat was founded in 2020 with the objective of providing rider a piece of mind when engaged in transportation system in indy go. We envision our initial product of take a seat being a launch point into variety of other customer relation improving software and hardware. Take a seat marketing offer is designed to improve the public health of Hoosier engaging in transportation service in Indy such as Indygo.

Cities are currently designed and run in a way that incentives individual mobility. This has resulted in systems that equate cars as equal to buses and have resulted in a variety of challenges. In the current climate of increasing populations, and climate change we believe it is becoming not only necessary but vital that cities redesign their transportation arrangements into a more intelligent, green, and efficient model. We hope that our current product will act as a stepping stool to allowing us to gain a voice, trust,and customer base so that we can face these new transportation challenges.
